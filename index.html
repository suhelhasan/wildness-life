Fantastic Animals

Fox..

Foxes are mammalian and omnivorous animals belonging to the Canidae family. They are medium-sized vulpids, characterized by a long snout and a long, hairy tail.

They also have their oval pupils, which are similar to the felids' vertical pupils.

Of about 40 species recognized as foxes, only 12 belong to the Vulpes genus of "true foxes", of which the red fox is the most common.

They also have their oval pupils, which are similar to the felids' vertical pupils.
